Peaky Blinders is a British crime show about the Shelby family after World War I in Birmingham, England. Thomas Shelby, who is smart and brave, leads the gang as they grow their power through business, politics, and hard work. Their ambition and loyalty make them one of the most feared families in the city.

The series is well-known for its bleak atmosphere, fashionable garments, and powerful narratives. The struggle in each season includes the battle between right and wrong and ambition, with Thomas Shelby struggling to balance his crime trade and desire for respect and tranquility. Peaky Blinders captivated millions with its history, drama, and iconic characters.
