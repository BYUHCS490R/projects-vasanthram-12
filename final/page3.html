Anatomy of an Android: Thirium and Biocomponents

The androids later on are an absolute technical feat. Their looks are nearly human because of very advanced artificial skin and hair. Under their hood, they use 'Thirium' called ('blue blood') to energize their synthetic biocomponents.

If a robot is broken, the blue blood is usually the only indication that the character is not a human. Such a tech provokes big questions about the source of life and awareness.
