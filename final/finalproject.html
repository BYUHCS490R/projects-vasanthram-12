Welcome to Detroit 2038

Meet the world of tomorrow when there are highly advanced androids that after a while start to behave like humans and even request their liberty. Detroit Become Human is a story, based game which delve into philosophical themes such as being, discrimination, and the essence of life.

Our platform uncovers the intricate narrative, the innovative technology, and the key characters struggling to survive in a world that is scared of them.
